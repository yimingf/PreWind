About

Before showing what we are really doing (or are we?) I want to tell some anecdotes to you.
This is a good project inherited from WINDMIND 2016.
We wanted to entitle it WINDMIND 2017 but I don't know if the existence of number will be bad-looking.
Have you noticed the letters of 'WINDMIND' are all capitalized?
Actually it had been a time, when all three capitalizations, say 'WindMind', 'windmind' and even 'Wind Mind' exist on all of the webpages.
I unified all capitalizations before some capitalization police start to arrest me.
Well I must stop to tell something 'related' to our project.
Imagine you are a trader in a wind power company, or at least some groups living on the magic power of wind.
How do you manage to make more money for your boss?
By standing on the baren shore feeling the wind?
If I were you (well I don't like the job though) I would wish to sit in my cozy office.
I wish I could watch the wind grow on the left side of the screen while watching Rick and Morty on the right side of the screen.
We call it meaningless life, or 'Babadoobie! Yabadabadada', or the motivation of our project.
Of course this project wouldn't make money for anybody.
It's just a bunch of pixels showing how a bunch of people who didn't know each other ever before could have done.
In two months.
Hope you like it, or hate it, or forget it after 5 minutes, which is none of my business.

-Yiming, 7/4/2017
